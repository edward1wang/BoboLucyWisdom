# Stanley Meyer

American inventor (1940-1998) who claimed to have developed a water fuel cell that could power vehicles using water instead of fuel.

## The Water Fuel Cell

- **Process:** Hydrogen fracturing via electron ionization
- **Claim:** Split water efficiently using high-voltage resonance
- **Demonstration:** Dune buggy ran on water (1990s)

## Death (1998)

Died of cerebral aneurysm after drinking grapefruit juice at dinner with Belgian investors.

**Controversy:**
- Official cause: natural
- Supporters claim: poisoned
- Circumstances: convenient timing after major funding deal

## Legacy

- Patents: US 4,936,961
- Never independently replicated
- Icon of suppressed technology movement

## Skeptic View

- Claims violate laws of thermodynamics
- No scientific peer review
- No working device exists
