# Thomas Townsend Brown

American physicist (1905-1985). Pioneer of electrogravitics — claimed high-voltage capacitors produce anti-gravity effects.

## Key Discovery

**The Biefeld-Brown Effect:**
- 1920s discovery as student
- High-voltage capacitors thrust toward positive plate
- Claimed electro-gravitational coupling

## Navy Career

- **Philadelphia Experiment survivor** (claimed)
- Worked on classified projects
- Disappeared from public view

## Anti-Gravity Claims

- Developed classified propulsion systems
- Electrogravitics: electrical control of gravity
- Tech allegedly exists in black projects
