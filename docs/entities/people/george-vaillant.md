# George Vaillant

Psychiatrist and researcher who directed the **Harvard Study of Adult Development** for over 30 years, analyzing 80 years of longitudinal data on happiness and health.

## Key Contributions

- Led the world's longest study of adult life
- Identified relationships as #1 predictor of longevity
- Challenged societal focus on wealth/status as health predictors

## Famous Quote

> "The only thing that matters in life is your relationships."

## Work

**Book**: *Triumphs of Experience* — summarizes the 80-year Harvard study findings
