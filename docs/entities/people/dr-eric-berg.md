# Dr. Eric Berg

Chiropractor and nutrition educator with massive online following. Known for **ketogenic diet expertise** and **high-dose vitamin D protocols**.

## Key Contributions

**Vitamin D3 Protocol:**
- Therapeutic: 20,000-50,000 IU × 14 days
- Maintenance: 5,000-10,000 IU ongoing
- **Mandatory cofactors:** K2 + magnesium (+ zinc optional)
