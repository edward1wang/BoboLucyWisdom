# Gary Brecka

Biohacker and functional medicine advocate focused on **micronutrient optimization** and root-cause diagnostics.

## Core Message

Chronic fatigue is rarely a sleep problem—it's often **micronutrient deficiency**.

## The Big Four

He emphasizes testing for:
1. Magnesium
2. Vitamin D
3. Vitamin B12
4. Ferritin

## Philosophy

> "Don't guess. Test. A simple blood panel can change everything."

- Advocates *optimal* ranges, not "normal" population averages
- Focus on measurable biomarkers
- Criticizes symptom-masking vs root cause treatment
