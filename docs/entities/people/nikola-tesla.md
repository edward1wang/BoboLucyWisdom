# Nikola Tesla

Serbian-American inventor, engineer, and futurist (1856-1943). Pioneer of alternating current electrical systems.

## Key Contributions

- **AC electrical system** — global standard
- **Induction motor** — without brushes
- **Tesla coil** — resonant transformer
- **Wireless transmission** — Wardenclyffe Tower

## Free Energy Work

- **Radiant energy** — cosmic rays
- **Wireless power** — global distribution
- **Magnifying transmitter** — Free energy claims

## Suppression

- Wardenclyffe Tower: funding cut by J.P. Morgan
- Lab fire (1895): destroyed early work
- FBI seized papers after death

## Death Ray Claims

Allegedly developed directed energy weapon (Tesla's words: "teleforce"). FBI files remain classified.
