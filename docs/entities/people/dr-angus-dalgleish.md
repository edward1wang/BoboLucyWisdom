# Dr. Angus Dalgleish

British oncologist and immunologist, one of the UK's most respected cancer researchers. Early proponent of **vitamin D in cancer immunotherapy**.

## Key Claims

**Vitamin D and Immunotherapy:**
- Patients with low vitamin D responded poorly to immunotherapy
- After vitamin D supplementation: response rates improved
- Insists vitamin D should be optimized *before* cancer treatment

## Critique of Healthcare

> "These bureaucratic medical organizations are idiots... If Pfizer invented a new drug that was expensive, toxic, and only worked for a few, they'd be thrilled. But they're completely uninterested in cheap, effective, non-toxic health solutions."

## Controversy

His views challenge the pharmaceutical-driven model of oncology:
- Advocates for low-cost interventions first
- Criticizes dismissal of nutritional approaches
- Calls for evidence-based *practical* medicine
