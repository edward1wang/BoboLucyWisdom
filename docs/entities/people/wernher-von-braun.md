# Wernher von Braun

German aerospace engineer (1912-1977). Led Nazi Germany's rocket program, then became chief architect of US space program.

## Career

| Era | Role | Achievement |
|-----|------|-------------|
| 1937-1945 | Nazi SS officer | V-2 rocket development |
| 1945-1959 | US Army | Ballistic missile program |
| 1960-1972 | NASA | Saturn V, Apollo program |

## Controversy

- Member of Nazi SS (Schutzstaffel)
- Used concentration camp slave labor (Nordhausen)
- Whitewashed history via Operation Paperclip
- Became "Father of American space travel"

## Legacy

- Every major rocket since V2 traces to his work
- SpaceX Falcon engines: lineage from V2 via J-2, Space Shuttle
