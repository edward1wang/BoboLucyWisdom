# Otto Warburg

German biochemist who won the **Nobel Prize (1931)** for discovering the metabolic basis of cancer—the "Warburg effect."

## Key Discovery

**Warburg Effect**:
- Cancer cells have damaged mitochondria
- They rely on **fermentation of glucose** (even with oxygen present)
- Cannot metabolize alternative fuels like ketones
- This metabolic rigidity can be exploited therapeutically

## Why Suppressed

His findings were largely ignored for a century because:
- Metabolic therapy is **unpatentable**
- No pharmaceutical profit motive
- Medical establishment focused on genetic/surgical approaches

## Quote

His work is now being revived as the **metabolic theory of cancer** gains recognition.
