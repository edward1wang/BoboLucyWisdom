# Testosterone Optimization

Natural methods to maintain healthy testosterone levels without pharmaceutical intervention.

## Why It Matters

Low testosterone associated with:
- Chronic fatigue
- Low libido and ED
- Muscle loss / fat gain
- Depression and cognitive decline
- Cardiovascular disease risk

## Natural Boosters

| Strategy | Mechanism | Evidence |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| **Sleep 7-9h** | T synthesis peaks during deep sleep | 5h/night drops T 10-15% |
| **Resistance training** | Acute + chronic T elevation | Compound lifts optimal |
| **Eliminate seed oils** | Reduce omega-6 inflammation | Seed oils inflammatory |
| **Avoid plastics/BPA** | Endocrine disruption | BPA suppresses LH |
| **Sunlight 10-20min** | Vitamin D synthesis | D = T precursor |
| **Vitamin D3** | >4000 IU for >12 weeks | Meta-analysis confirmed |
| **Zinc 30-60mg** | T synthesis cofactor | Short term only |
| **Boron 6-10mg** | Lowers SHBG | Frees up bioavailable T |

## What Lowers T

- Sleep deprivation
- Chronic stress (cortisol)
- Excess body fat (aromatization)
- Alcohol
- Endocrine disruptors (BPA, phthalates)
- Overtraining
