# Chronic Fatigue and Micronutrient Deficiency

Most people assume fatigue is a sleep problem. Often it's not. Even 8 hours of sleep can leave you exhausted if you're deficient in key micronutrients.

## The Big Four Deficiencies

| Nutrient | Test | Role |
|----------|------|------|
| **Magnesium** | Serum/RBC magnesium | Energy production, muscle function |
| **Vitamin D** | 25(OH)D | Brain health, immunity, energy |
| **Vitamin B12** | Serum B12 | Nerve function, energy metabolism |
| **Ferritin** | Serum ferritin | Iron storage, oxygen transport |

## The Testing Principle

> "Don't guess. Test. A simple blood panel can change everything."

— Gary Brecka

## Why Deficiencies Go Unnoticed

- Symptoms (fatigue, weakness) are non-specific
- Standard panels often *don't* include these
- "Normal" ranges are based on population averages, not optimal function

## Action Steps

1. Request complete micronutrient panel
2. Check levels against *optimal* (not just normal)
3. Supplement specifically based on results
4. Retest every 3-6 months
