# Dementia Prevention

Alzheimer's and cognitive decline are not inevitable. Multiple modifiable risk factors.

## Evidence-Based Strategies

| Factor | Action | Evidence |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **Coffee** | 3 cups/day | Harvard: 43 years, 18% risk reduction |
| **Vitamin D** | Maintain 50-80 ng/mL | Mid-life D predicts later risk |
| **Exercise** | 150 min/week moderate | Increases BDNF, blood flow |
| **Sleep** | 7-9 hours | Clears amyloid during deep sleep |
| **Social** | Maintain relationships | Harvard 80-year study |
| **Heart health** | Control BP, cholesterol | Vascular dementia link |
| **Avoid fructose** | Limit sugar | Linked to Alzheimer's |

## Early Signs

- Forgetting recent conversations
- Difficulty finding words
- Getting lost in familiar places
- Changes in personality
