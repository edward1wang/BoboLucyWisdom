# Supplement Timing

Taking vitamins at optimal times significantly improves absorption and utilization.

## Morning (With Fat)

| Supplement | Timing | Reason |
|------------|--------|--------|
| Vitamin D | Morning + fat | Fat-soluble, energizing |
| Omega-3 | Morning + fat | Fat-soluble, anti-inflammatory |
| Vitamin B12 | Morning, with or without food | Energy support |

## Evening

| Supplement | Timing | Reason |
|------------|--------|--------|
| Magnesium | Evening | Promotes relaxation, improves sleep |

## Special Instructions

**Iron:**
- Take **with Vitamin C** (enhances absorption)
- Take on **empty stomach**
- Avoid coffee, tea, calcium (inhibit absorption)

**Flexible:**
- Calcium
- Zinc
- Selenium
- Vitamin C

## General Principles

1. **Fat-soluble vitamins** (D, E, K, A) need dietary fat
2. **Minerals compete** — don't take calcium + zinc + iron together
3. **Consistency beats perfection** — take daily at same time
4. **Blood test first** — know your deficiencies
