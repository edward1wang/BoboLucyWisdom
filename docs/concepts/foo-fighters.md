# Foo Fighters

Glowing orbs appearing during WWII aerial combat (1940s). Both Axis and Allied pilots reported them.

## Characteristics

- Spherical energy fields
- Solid metal shells (reflected sunlight)
- **Interdimensional capability:** Could phase through aircraft
- Electromagnetic interference (disrupted radar, compasses)

## Official Response

- **Jimmy Doolittle investigation:** Reported to FDR as "interplanetary craft"
- National security concerns began immediately
- No explanation offered to public

## Theories

| Theory | Evidence |
|--------|----------|
| German secret weapon | Both sides observed them |
| Allied secret weapon | Same issue |
| Extraterrestrial | Phasing capability |
| Natural phenomenon | EM effects unlikely |

## Significance

First widely-documented UFO phenomenon in modern military context. Led to establishment of secret investigation programs.
