# Victim Mentality and Agency

Charlie Munger: "Whenever you feel a situation or someone is ruining your life, actually **you are ruining your life**. Seeing yourself as a victim is a catastrophic way to ruin your life."

## The Trap

| Victim Mindset | Agency Mindset |
|----------------|----------------|
| "The market caused my investment failure" | "I made choices that led to this outcome" |
| External blame | Internal responsibility |
| Repeats mistakes | Learns and adapts |

## Domains Affected

- Investments → Blame markets, repeat errors
- Marriage → Blame partner, no growth
- Career → Blame economy/boss, stay stuck
- Relationships → Blame others, isolate

## The Shift

**Key insight:** Reclaiming agency requires stopping blame and accepting responsibility—even for things not "your fault."

> "The path to regaining control of your life is to stop blaming others, stop the victim mindset, and take responsibility."
