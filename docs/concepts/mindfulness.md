# Mindfulness

Present-moment awareness without judgment. Foundation of mental clarity and emotional regulation.

## Core Practice

- **Attention**: Focus on present experience
- **Attitude**: Non-judgmental, curious
- **Awareness**: Open to whatever arises

## Benefits

- Reduced rumination
- Lower cortisol (stress hormone)
- Improved focus
- Better emotional regulation
- Reduced anxiety

## vs. Meditation

| Meditation | Mindfulness |
|------------|-------------|
| Formal practice | Can be anytime |
| Specific technique | Way of being |
| Sitting practice | Walking, eating, breathing |

## Practical Application

- Mindful breathing
- Body awareness
- Eating mindfully
- Walking meditation
