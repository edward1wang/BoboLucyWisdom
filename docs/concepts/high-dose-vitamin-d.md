# High-Dose Vitamin D3 Therapy

**Dr. Eric Berg's protocol**: 20,000-50,000 IU daily for 14 days as therapeutic intervention, not just maintenance.

## Therapeutic Protocol

| Phase | Dose | Duration |
|-------|------|----------|
| Therapeutic | 20,000-50,000 IU/day | 14 days |
| Maintenance | 5,000-10,000 IU/day | Ongoing |

## Reported Benefits

- Enhanced immunity
- Reduced inflammation
- Depression relief
- Blood pressure reduction
- Autoimmune condition improvement

## Critical Cofactors

**MUST accompany high-dose D3:**
- **Vitamin K2** (directs calcium to bones, not arteries)
- **Magnesium** (required for D activation)
- **Zinc** (immune synergy)

**Risks if omitted:**
- Calcium mismanagement
- Arterial calcification
- Toxicity symptoms

## Monitoring

- Test 25(OH)D every 3-6 months
- Target: 50-80 ng/mL
- Don't exceed without medical supervision
