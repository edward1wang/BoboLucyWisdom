# Zero-Point Energy

Energy inherent in the quantum vacuum — the "empty" space between particles. Potentially infinite energy source suppressed by energy industry.

## Key Concepts

- **Quantum vacuum:** Not empty — seething with virtual particles
- **Dipole tapping:** Generators create dipoles to access cosmic electromagnetic energy
- **ECE Theory:** Myron Evans' unified field theory

| Era | Progress | Suppression |

## Suppression Mechanism

- **Eugene Mallove:** MIT cold fusion researcher, mysteriously died after breakthrough
- **Stanley Meyer:** Water fuel cell inventor, allegedly poisoned
- **Academic gatekeeping:** Publish-or-die system rejects anomalous results

If deployed: end of energy scarcity, petrodollar collapse, geopolitical restructuring
