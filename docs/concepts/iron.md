# Iron

Essential mineral for oxygen transport (hemoglobin) and energy production.

## Functions

- Hemoglobin synthesis (oxygen transport)
- Myoglobin (muscle oxygen storage)
- Energy metabolism
- Immune function

## Deficiency Symptoms

- Fatigue, weakness
- Pale skin
- Shortness of breath
- Dizziness
- Cold hands/feet

## Testing

- **Ferritin**: Best measure of iron stores
- Serum iron: Immediate levels
- TIBC: Total iron binding capacity

## Supplementation Protocol

- Take with **vitamin C** (enhances absorption)
- Take on **empty stomach**
- Avoid: coffee, tea, calcium (inhibit absorption)
- Typical dose: 18-65mg elemental iron
