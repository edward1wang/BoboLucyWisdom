# Hair Loss Reversal Protocol

Harvard research (2021) showing that **hair follicle stem cells remain present even in bald scalps**—they're dormant, not dead. Proper stimulation, nutrition, and oxygen can reactivate them.

## Key Mechanism

- DHT (dihydrotestosterone) causes follicles to shrink
- Gas6 protein activates follicle stem cells
- Stress hormones block Gas6

## The 5-Step Protocol

| Step | Action | Evidence |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Daily scalp massage (5-10 min) | 2016 study: 24 weeks → denser hair |
| 2 | Hydration (2.5-3.5L water daily) | Prevents nutrient/oxygen deprivation |
| 3 | DHT blocking | Finasteride, pumpkin seed oil, saw palmetto, green tea extract |
| 4 | Stress reduction | Sleep 7-8h, exercise, meditation → restore Gas6 |
| 5 | Nutrition | Protein, iron, zinc, vitamin C |

## Bonus: Testosterone Optimization

Low testosterone linked to fatigue, ED, muscle loss, depression, and cardiovascular risk.

**Natural boosters:**
- Sleep 7-9 hours (5 hours/night drops T 10-15%)
- Resistance training 2-3x/week (compound lifts)
- Eliminate seed oils + plastics (endocrine disruptors)
- Morning sunlight 10-20 min
- Vitamin D (>4000 IU/day for >12 weeks)
- Zinc 30-60mg/day (short term)
- Boron 6-10mg/day (reduces SHBG)
