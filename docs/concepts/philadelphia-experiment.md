# Philadelphia Experiment

Alleged 1943 US Navy experiment to make USS Eldridge invisible to radar. Claim involving teleportation, time travel, human casualties.

## The Claim

- **Date:** October 28, 1943
- **Location:** Philadelphia Naval Shipyard
- **Method:** Einstein-unified field theory + high-voltage Tesla coils
- **Goal:** Radar invisibility (stealth)

## Alleged Results

- Ship briefly disappeared
- Teleported to Norfolk, Virginia and back
- Crew suffered severe physical effects
- Some embedded in ship's hull

## Debunking

- No USS Eldridge log entries for October 1943
- USS Eldridge was in New York, not Philadelphia
- "Witnesses" never existed
- Physics violates current understanding

## Supporters' Response

- Records classified/destroyed
- Survivors silenced
- True nature of experiment more exotic than claimed

## Connection to Townsend Brown

He allegedly witnessed experiment, then worked on classified follow-up projects.
