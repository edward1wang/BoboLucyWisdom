# Free Energy Suppression

Claim that working over-unity energy devices exist but are suppressed by powerful interests.

## Suppression Patterns

| Researcher | Discovery | Fate |
|------------|-----------|------|
| **Eugene Mallove** | Cold fusion vindication | Brutally murdered (2004) |
| **Stanley Meyer** | Water fuel cell | Allegedly poisoned (1998) |
| **Nikola Tesla** | Free energy transmission | Lab burned, funding cut |
| **Thomas Moray** | Radiant energy device | Threatened, went silent |

## Mechanisms

- **Academic gatekeeping:** Peer review rejects anomalies
- **Physical threats:** Inventors attacked or disappeared
- **Economic sabotage:** No funding, blacklisted
- **Media control:** "Impossible" narrative

## Why Suppress?

- $10+ trillion annual energy economy
- Petrodollar system collapse
- Control over population via scarcity

## Skeptic View

- No working device independently verified
- Conspiracy theory with no physical evidence
- Violates laws of thermodynamics
