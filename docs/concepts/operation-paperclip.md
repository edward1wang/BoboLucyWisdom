# Operation Paperclip

US program (1945-1959) that recruited 1,600+ German scientists, engineers, and technicians — including Nazi party members — to work for American government.

## Key Claims (from video)

- **Not about infiltration** — scientists traded knowledge for amnesty
- Race against USSR to acquire German tech talent
- Found alien spacecraft in Nazi hangars post-war

## Notable Recruits

| Scientist | Nazi Role | US Role |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| **Wernher von Braun** | V2 rocket developer | NASA Apollo architect |
| **Hubertus Strughold** | Luftwaffe researcher | Space medicine pioneer |
