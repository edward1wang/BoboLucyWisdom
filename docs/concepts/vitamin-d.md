# Vitamin D

Critical hormone (not just a vitamin) involved in brain health, immunity, bone health, and longevity. ~1 billion people globally are deficient.

## Brain Health (Dementia Prevention)

**16-year study findings:**
- Mid-life vitamin D levels predict later dementia risk
- Adequate D = less Tau protein buildup (Alzheimer's marker)
- Mechanism: activates microglia (brain's cleanup crew)

## Cancer & Immunity

Dr. Angus Dalgleish (oncologist): Vitamin D supplementation improves immunotherapy response rates. Calls it "cheap, effective, non-toxic" vs expensive, toxic drugs.

## Optimal Levels

| Status | 25(OH)D Level |
|--------|---------------|
| Deficient | <20 ng/mL |
| Adequate | 30-50 ng/mL |
| Optimal | 50-80 ng/mL |

## Supplementation Protocol

**If deficient:**
- 5000-10000 IU D3 short-term (medical supervision)
- Maintenance: 2000-5000 IU D3 daily

**Must combine with:**
- **Vitamin K2** (directs calcium to bones, not arteries)
- **Magnesium** (required to activate D)
- Take with fatty meal (fat-soluble)

## Sunlight vs Supplements

**Natural synthesis:**
- UVB exposure triggers skin production
- 15-30 min midday sun = 10,000-20,000 IU
- Sunscreen blocks UVB → blocks D synthesis

**Strategy:** Get 20-30 min unprotected sun, *then* apply sunscreen if needed.
