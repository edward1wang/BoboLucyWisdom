# Aether (Luminiferous Aether)

Historical concept: medium filling all space, allowing light to propagate.

## Historical Timeline

| Era | Status |
|-----|--------|
| Ancient Greece | Philosophical concept |
| 19th Century | Scientific consensus |
| 1887 Michelson-Morley | Experiment found no aether |
| 1905 Einstein | Special relativity: space empty |
| Modern | Dismissed by mainstream |

## Supporters' View

- **Tesla:** "I have worked out a dynamic theory of gravity in all details... the assumption of this medium (aether) is indispensable"
- **Modern:** Scientists privately acknowledge but publicly avoid discussing
- **Comparison:** Like medieval church suppressing heliocentrism

## In Energy Physics

- Medium for zero-point energy
- Carrier of scalar waves
- Cosmic energy source

## Why Dismissed?

- Michelson-Morley null result
- Relativity works without it
- Career suicide to research
