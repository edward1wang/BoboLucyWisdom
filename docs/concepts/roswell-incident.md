# Roswell Incident (1947)

July 1947: Object crashed on ranch near Roswell, New Mexico. Most famous UFO incident in history.

## The Claim

- Flying saucer crashed
- Alien bodies recovered
- Technology reverse-engineered
- Military cover-up

## Official Story (Changes)

| Date | Explanation |
|------|-------------|
| 1947 | "Flying disc crashed" — initial report |
| Later | Weather balloon |
| 1994 | Mogul spy balloon project |
| 1997 | Test dummies (anthropomorphic) |

## Why Roswell?

- Only place in world with atomic weapons (1947)
- Heavy military presence
- Remote location

## Reverse Engineering Claims

**Colonel Philip Corso** (*The Day After Roswell*):
- Fiber optics from saucer
- Integrated circuits (ICs)
- Night vision
- Kevlar
