# Magnesium

Essential mineral involved in 300+ biochemical reactions. Critical for energy production, muscle function, nerve transmission, and sleep quality.

## Key Functions

- **Energy production**: Required for ATP synthesis
- **Muscle relaxation**: Counteracts calcium contraction signals
- **Nerve function**: Regulates neurotransmitter signaling
- **Sleep quality**: Required for GABA receptor function
- **Stress response**: Regulates HPA axis

## Deficiency Symptoms

- Muscle cramps and twitches
- Fatigue and weakness
- Insomnia / poor sleep
- Anxiety and irritability
- High blood pressure

## Food Sources

- Leafy greens (spinach, kale)
- Nuts and seeds (almonds, pumpkin seeds)
- Dark chocolate
- Avocado
- Legumes

## Supplementation

| Form | Absorption | Best For |
|------|------------|----------|
| Magnesium glycinate | High | Sleep, anxiety |
| Magnesium citrate | High | Constipation |
| Magnesium threonate | Very high | Brain health |
| Magnesium oxide | Low | Cost only |
