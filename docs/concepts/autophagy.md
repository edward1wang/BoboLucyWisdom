# Autophagy

Cellular "self-eating" process. Cells recycle damaged components during nutrient scarcity.

## Mechanism

- Triggered by: fasting, exercise, ketosis
- mTOR (growth) ↓ → autophagy (cleanup) ↑
- Lysosomes degrade and recycle cellular debris

## Benefits

- Removes damaged mitochondria
- Clears misfolded proteins
- May slow aging
- Reduces inflammation

## How to Induce

| Method | Duration Needed |
|--------|-----------------|
| Water fasting | 16-24+ hours |
| Keto diet | Sustained ketosis |
| Exercise | Regular training |
| CR mimetics | Supplements (caution) |

## Relation to Fasting

Intermittent fasting triggers autophagy in liver, muscle, and brain.
