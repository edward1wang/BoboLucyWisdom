# DHT (Dihydrotestosterone)

Androgen hormone derived from testosterone. Primary driver of male pattern baldness and prostate enlargement.

## Mechanism

- 5-alpha reductase converts testosterone → DHT
- 3x more potent than testosterone
- Binds to follicle androgen receptors → follicle miniaturization

## Effects

| Beneficial | Problematic |
|------------|-------------|
| Body/facial hair growth | Scalp hair loss |
| Voice deepening | Prostate enlargement |
| Muscle development | Acne |
| Libido | Aggression |
