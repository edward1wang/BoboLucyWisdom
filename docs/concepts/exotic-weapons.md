# Exotic Weapons

Classified advanced weapons allegedly developed by military-industrial complex beyond conventional warfare.

## Types

| Weapon | Description | Status |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| **Death rays** | Directed energy beams | Tesla claimed success |
| **Scalar waves** | Non-Hertzian waves, Tesla concept | Theoretical/contested |
| **Force fields** | Energy barriers | Sci-fi, no evidence |
| **Weather modification** | HAARP-like ionospheric heating | Documented, limited |
| **Mind control** | EM/radio wave neurological effects | Research stage |

## Tesla's Death Ray

- Claimed ability to down aircraft at 200+ miles
- Allegedly demonstrated to US Military
- Never publicly verified

## Theoretical Basis

- Tesla's 1930s "teleforce" patents
- Scalar electromagnetics
- Zero-point energy weapons

## Skeptic View

- Extraordinary claims require extraordinary evidence
- No surviving Tesla prototypes
- Cold War never deployed "death rays"
