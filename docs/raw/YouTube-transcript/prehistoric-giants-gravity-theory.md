# Prehistoric Giants: Questioning Dinosaur Extinction & Gravity

## Summary
Chinese documentary examining problems with dinosaur biology under current gravity, proposing Earth's gravity was weaker in prehistoric times.

---

## Scientific Problems Raised

### Dinosaur Scale Issue
- **Largest sauropod:** 60-70m long, estimated 180 tons
- **Problem:** Cannot support weight under current Earth gravity
- Previous hypotheses: Lived in water OR in swampy/muddy land
- **Current consensus:** Lived on solid ground (footprint evidence)

### Mainstream Extinction Theory
- Asteroid/comet impact
- Volcanic activity lasting centuries
- **Mechanism:** Dust blocks sunlight → reduces photosynthesis → ecosystem collapse
- **Criticism:** "Only collisions and explosions explain everything"

### Gravity Variation Hypothesis
- **Core claim:** Earth's gravity was weaker during dinosaur era
- **Implication:** Giants could exist, move, survive
- **Suggests:** Catastrophic change increased gravity → caused extinction

---

## Biological Classifications Clarified
**True dinosaurs (land reptiles):**
- Herbivores: Brachiosaurus, Diplodocus, Stegosaurus
- Carnivores: T-Rex, Velociraptor

**Not dinosaurs (archosaurs):**
- Pterosaurs, Plesiosaurs, Mosasaurs, Ichthyosaurs, Dimetrodon

## Key Evidence
- Footprint fossils more common than bone fossils
- Sauropod tracks found globally (solid ground)
- Largest footprint evidence: 60-70m creature
