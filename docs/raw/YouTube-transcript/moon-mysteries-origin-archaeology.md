# Moon Mysteries: Challenging Mainstream Origin Theories

## Summary
Chinese documentary examining evidence contradicting the "giant impact" theory of lunar formation, proposing alternative origins involving ancient intelligent activity.

---

## Key Scientific Anomalies

### South Pole-Aitken Basin
- **Size:** Largest, oldest impact crater in solar system
- **Scale:** Mount Everest would fit with 4000m to spare
- **Mystery:** Impact didn't break moon apart—suggests unusual internal structure

### Carbon Ion Discovery
- **Source:** Japanese JAXA lunar probe (ion mass spectrometer)
- **Finding:** Widespread carbon ions across lunar surface, high concentration
- **Problem:** Giant impact theory predicts 4000-6000K temperatures
- **Implication:** Should have vaporized all volatiles including carbon
- **Contradiction:** Carbon abundance contradicts standard collision model

### Mainstream Theory Problems
- **Theia hypothesis:** Mars-sized body hit Earth → debris formed Moon
- **Required temperature:** 3700-5700°C
- **Predicted result:** Volatile-depleted "dry" Moon
- **Actual findings:** Volatiles present (water, carbon)
