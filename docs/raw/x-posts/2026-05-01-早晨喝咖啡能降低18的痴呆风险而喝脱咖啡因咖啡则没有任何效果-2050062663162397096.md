早晨喝咖啡能降低18%的痴呆风险。而喝脱咖啡因咖啡则没有任何效果。哈佛大学对131821人进行了长达43年的追踪研究，以探究其中的原因。 咖啡因是腺苷拮抗剂。腺苷是一种会在白天于大脑中积累并导致困倦的分子。它还会抑制参与记忆形成的神经递质。咖啡因会在腺苷与受体结合之前将其阻断。因此，记忆回路受到的抑制程度会降低。 这就是脱咖啡因咖啡失败的原因。脱咖啡因咖啡和普通咖啡含有相同的多酚、相同的抗氧化剂和相同的镁。唯一缺少的是阻断腺苷受体的分子。如果多酚起作用，脱咖啡因咖啡应该会显示出部分效果。但实际上，它完全没有效果。 最有趣的是：即使是那些具有较高痴呆症遗传风险的人群，咖啡也具有保护作用。基因就像一颗定时炸弹，咖啡则能减缓子弹的落下速度。 咖啡因摄入量与咖啡因摄入量之间的关系曲线在三杯左右趋于平缓。五杯的效果并不比三杯好。超过饱和状态后，继续喝咖啡只会让你感到紧张不安，没有任何其他益处。 咖啡是全球消费量最大的精神活性药物，每天消耗20亿杯。几十年来，我们一直把它当作一种轻微的嗜好。结果却发现，它竟然是人类历史上规模最大的意外痴呆症预防项目。 黑咖啡、柠檬水、绿茶，就是我的饮品，你呢？ ～关注我，一起探索自愈之路～ Aakash Gupta (@aakashgupta) Your morning coffee cuts dementia risk by 18%. Decaf does nothing. Harvard tracked 131,821 people for 43 years to figure out why. Caffeine is an adenosine antagonist. Adenosine is the molecule that builds up in your brain through the day and makes you sleepy. It also dampens the neurotransmitters involved in memory formation. Caffeine blocks the receptor before adenosine can lock in. Memory circuits run less suppressed than they would otherwise. That's why decaf failed. Decaf has the same polyphenols as regular coffee. Same antioxidants. Same magnesium. The only thing missing is the molecule that blocks the adenosine receptor. If polyphenols were doing the work, decaf would have shown a partial effect. It showed zero. The most interesting part: the protective effect held even in people with high genetic risk for dementia. Genes load the gun. Coffee slows the trigger pull. The dose-response curve plateaus around 3 cups. Five cups didn't beat three. Past saturation, additional coffee buys you jitters and nothing else. Coffee is the most consumed psychoactive drug on the planet. Two billion cups per day. We've spent decades treating it like a mild vice. Turns out it's the largest accidental dementia prevention program in human history. — https://nitter.net/aakashgupta/status/2049671091686416595`#m`
