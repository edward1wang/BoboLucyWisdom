    Source: https://youtu.be/jmBUphTrS54

    Language: Chinese audio with transcript below

    Description¶

    Electric universe perspective on fossil formation. Argues that fossilization occurs rapidly through electrical discharge rather than slow geological processes. Evidence from petrified trees, fossilized embryos, and mineral deposits.
